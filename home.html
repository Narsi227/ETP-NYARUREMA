About Us

École Technique Paroissiale de Nyarurema (ETP Nyarurema) is a public TVET school located in Nyagatare District, Eastern Province, Rwanda.

We are committed to empowering youth through quality technical and vocational education, preparing them for employment, entrepreneurship, and self-reliance.

Our vision aligns with Rwanda’s National Strategy for Transformation — building competent, skilled citizens ready for the labor market and innovation.

As a faith-based institution under the Catholic parish of Nyarurema, we emphasize values of integrity, hard work, and service to community.

Trades at ETP Nyarurema

Our TVET programs are designed to provide practical, industry-relevant skills in high-demand trades.

Our Main Trades

Software Development (SOD)

Programming, web/mobile development, databases, and software engineering skills for the digital economy.

Automobile Technology (AUT)

Vehicle diagnostics, repair, maintenance, and modern automotive systems training.

Building & Construction (BDC)

Masonry, carpentry, plumbing, electrical installation, and sustainable construction practices.

Accounting (ACC)

Financial accounting, bookkeeping, taxation, auditing, and business finance preparation.

Important Admission Notes (TVET Levels):

  • Level 3 (L3) entry: For students who have completed S3 (Senior 3 / O-Level). This is the main entry point after Lower Secondary.
  • Level 4 / Level 5: Requires completion of previous TVET level (e.g., L3 for L4, L4 for L5) or equivalent qualifications.
  • Send your report cards/transcripts from lower levels (S3/S4 results, previous certificates) to support your application.
  • After submission, email scanned copies of your report cards/certificates to nightnarsi@gmail.com with your full name and preferred trade.

Note: Please email scanned copies of previous education to nightnarsi@gmail.com after submitting, including your name and trade/level.
